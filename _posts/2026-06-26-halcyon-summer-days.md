The busyness of spring is over, and we have time to relax and enjoy these beautiful summer days. The garden is growing a lot, and so are all the baby animals! Kip and Annie love to watch over it all, ready for anything we need them to do. (And when the heat becomes annoying, they take a quick run down to the creek to cool off.)

![Lambs](/assets/images/kip/kip-with-lambs.jpg)

We are babysitting some lambs for a friend, and it's been fun to watch these farm dogs' introduction to sheep! When one lamb escaped from the pen, Annie rounded it up like she'd worked with sheep all her life. Kip likes to hang out beside the pen to make sure everything is as it should be.
