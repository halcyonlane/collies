Captain Jack

We started looking at Scotch Collies in 2012 after we moved to Halcyon Lane Farm. Captain Jack is one of the Heritage dogs and we were really excited to get in on a litter of Lassen and Rainier .

It was an adventure getting Captain Jack in August of 2012 when we made a trip to Missouri as part of a “puppy train” starting in Arizona and ending in Michigan.
