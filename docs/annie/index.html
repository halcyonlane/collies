The Annie Doc

When our dog Lassie died, we started looking for a new female and found Annie! From 4J Acres in Texas, picking her up was a highlight of a family vacation we took in 2021. from a cute and adorable puppy, she has grown up into a beautifull and remarkable dog. Annie loves looking after the farm, ecpecially keeping predators away from the chickens and helping herd any escaped animals back to their pens. She also loves playing fetch with anyone who will throw a stick or ball for her. Read more about Annie
