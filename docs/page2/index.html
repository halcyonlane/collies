Puppies at six weeks

Mar 28, 2026

Time is going by so fast, it’s crazy to think that we only have two more weeks until the puppies are ready to go to their new homes! We have so enjoyed having these puppies, but are excited for their new owners too. We have only one puppy available now, a male. If you are interested, contact me here: dahlia@halcyonlane.com

They still love to play outside, and with the beautiful spring weather we’ve been having, they get a lot of outside time!

The cats do a great job of putting up with puppy curiosity, but will put a puppy in their place, when they get too playful. :)

We have a new arrival on the farm, a baby goat was born this week! We had fun introducing him to the puppies.
