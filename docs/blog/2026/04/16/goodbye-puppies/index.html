Goodbye puppies

Apr 16, 2026

Linden went to his new family this week. We’ve said goodbye to all the puppies now, and they are gone to some really great new homes! We’ve been so impressed with this litter, and are hoping to have another litter of puppies by Annie and Kip next year, but we’ll see what the future holds… 😁

Having these puppies has been a great experience and it was so fun to meet people looking for the old time collie. Now we are back to just our two family dogs and that’s a good feeling too :)

Annie and Kip are playing alot again now. :)
